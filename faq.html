We recommend an eye examination every 1 to 3 years for adults. Some signs should raise an alert: headaches, visual fatigue, blurry vision either up close or far away, etc. Children should have an eye assessment at each major stage of their development.

Unsuitable contact lenses can lead to various complications: infections, corneal abrasions, conjunctivitis, etc. Our expertise guarantees safe contact lens wear.

There is no maximum age limit for wearing contact lenses if the condition of the cornea allows it. We perform a full examination to determine the suitable contact lens solution according to age.

Part of the contact lens package is covered by Health Insurance. Some insurance companies offer additional reimbursement upon providing a detailed invoice.

Progressive lenses have several benefits compared to traditional bifocal lenses:

  • - Clear vision both far and near without a separation line
  • - Gradual adaptation between the different vision zones
  • - Large intermediate vision zone (computer, dashboard...)
  • - Better aesthetics and discretion

Our opticians will guide you in choosing progressive lenses perfectly suited to your visual needs and activities.
